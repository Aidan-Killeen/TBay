Once the project is running:
1) Select the navbar option for Signup or Login
    1.1) Sign up using valid email and password combination
    1.2) Login using valid email and password combination

2) You will be redirected to the homepage
    2.1) Navigation bar should update its options accordingly
    2.2) View a list of products pulled from our firebase database

3) Select the pencil option in the navbar to create a new product
    2.1) Enter product details and hit the 'Sell' button
    2.2) This posts the new product to the database

4) Route to the homepage
    4.1) View the newly created product

4) Hit the logout button to logout
    4.1) Navigation bar should update its options accordingly
